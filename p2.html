It is easy to grow food in Mesopotamia because the soil here is so fertile and that is because of the mountains up north it snows a lot up there in the winter when the snow melts all that water runs down into the valley and into the rivers,
They grow barley and wheat and lots of beans and fresh vegetables and fruits like melons dates, apples, pigs and grapes.
Their animals give them milk, cheese and meat.
They invented a way to control the river.
First they learned how to build levees those are big piles of earth between the fields and the river they are too tall for the river to get over.
