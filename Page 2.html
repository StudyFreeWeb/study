About Mesopotamia

Mesopotamia is located in present day Iraq.
Mesopotamia is the land between two rivers.
The two rivers name call Tigris and Euphrates.
The first thing Sumerians did was get really good of farming.
Sumerians made weapons and they used them for hunting wild animals for foods.
And they discovered how to grow plants.
They also found out how to tame wild goats and sheep and raise for food that is call domestication.
